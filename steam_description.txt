[h1]Provides a Remix config for overriding most of slugcat's basic stats![/h1]

[b]These include food requirements, run speed, lung capacity, weight and a few more![/b]

[b]Unfortunately, certain stats, such as roll speed and slide distance, are hardcoded in the player script - these are beyond the scope of this mod.[/b]

[b][u]Does not seem to affect SlugBase characters for the most part.[/u][b]

[u][b]Source: https://github.com/forthbridge/slugcat-stats-config[/b][/u]
